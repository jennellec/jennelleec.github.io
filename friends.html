LETTER TO MY


FRIENDS




Dear Friends,

I'm glad I met kind people like you. I will always remember the fun times we had together. Thanks for cheering me up when I am feeling sad. Even though our time together is not that long, I still enjoyed every moment with you. I wish we could still be friends in the future even though we won't be seeing each other anymore. I love you guys and I hope we will not forget the friendship we had together.
