LETTER TO


GOD




Dear God,

I am thankful that you brought me into this world. I'm sorry for all the bad things I did. I love you God because I know that you love me more. Thank you for always accompanying me when I am alone. I appreciate all the blessings that you gave me even though I don't deserve it. I am also thankful for the unexpected things that happened in my life since I know you have a purpose for everything. I know you ae always watching over me and you are helping me when I am suffering.
