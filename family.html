LETTER TO MY


FAMILY




Dear Family,

I'm happy to be a part of this family. I'm thankful that we are together unlike some families whose members are far away. Thank you for supporting me in doing the things that want to do. When I have nowhere else to go, you always welcome me with open arms. I'm glad that we could spend most of the time to bond with each other. Thanks for taking care of me. I love you.
